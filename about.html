Klo Pelgag

ABOUT

Since her appearance at the dawn of 2013, Klo Pelgag has established herself as a sibylline breath of fresh air in the French-speaking musical landscape. The singer-songwriter becomes one of the most convincing and singular voices of her generation. Notre-dame-des-Sept-Douleurs, her third and most recent album, recounts an intrinsic journey that is sometimes suffocating, but powerfully emancipating and freeing. These songs belong to an unraveled universe and are probably the most limpid and personal work that the artist has offered, to date.

Depuis son apparition a l'oree des annes 2013, Klo Pelgag s'impose comme une sibylline bouffee d'air frais dans le paysage musical francophone. L'autrice-compositrice-interprete devient l'une des voix les plus probantes et singulieres de sa generation. Notre-dame-des-Sept-Douleurs, son troisieme et plus recent album, raconte un cheminement intrinseque tantot suffocant, mais puissament emancipateur et affranchissant. Ces chansons appartiennent a un univers denoue et constituent probablement l'oeuvre la plus limpide et personnelle que l'artiste ait offerte, jusqu'a ce jour.
